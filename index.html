Before You Reach Out

The questions clients usually ask, answered upfront.

What's your typical turnaround?
Most single short-form edits take 2 to 4 days. I'll confirm the exact delivery date before we start.
How do I send you my footage?
Google Drive, Dropbox, or Frame.io all work. Send raw clips, brand assets, and a quick note on the goal.
How many revisions are included?
Every project includes revisions until the cut lands right, typically 2 to 3 rounds.
What formats do you deliver in?
Vertical 9:16 for Reels, TikTok, and Shorts, plus square or widescreen when needed.
Do you work with clients in other time zones?
Yes. Communication stays clear and async-friendly, so time zones never slow the project down.
Can you match my existing style?
Absolutely. Send references or past videos and I'll match the pacing, tone, and look.
